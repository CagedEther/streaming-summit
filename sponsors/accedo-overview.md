# Accedo — Overview

> Your video business partner — Design, develop, deliver, and test world-class streaming experiences

## What They Do
Accedo is a specialist streaming software and services company — headquartered in Sweden with global delivery capability — that helps media and entertainment companies build, launch, optimize, and operate multi-screen OTT streaming services. Unlike platform vendors who provide middleware, Accedo sits at the intersection of strategy, design, engineering, and quality assurance: they will design the UX, build the apps across every device platform, integrate the technology stack, provide ongoing managed services, and monitor quality — all from a single partner relationship. Their 20+ year focus on OTT gives them arguably the deepest device-by-device streaming development expertise of any pure-play services firm in the market.

## Products & Services

### OTT Application Development
Custom and accelerated development of streaming applications across every major platform and device — Smart TVs (Samsung Tizen, LG webOS), connected devices (Apple TV, Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Chromecast), mobile (iOS, Android), web, and set-top boxes. Accedo builds native applications on each platform rather than cross-compiled solutions, ensuring optimal performance and platform-specific user experience conventions are respected. Device fragmentation management is a core competency: supporting legacy smart TV models that remain in households for years alongside current-generation hardware.

### Accedo Elevate
Accedo's flagship accelerated OTT application framework — described as combining "the quick time to market of ready-made OTT applications with all the customization benefits of bespoke video services." Elevate provides a modularized, extendable application architecture with pre-built UI components, user journey flows (authentication, search, browse, playback, payments), and integrations for major video technology providers, analytics tools, CDNs, and DRM systems. Clients can launch a customized, production-ready streaming service in weeks rather than months, and continue evolving it over time without rebuilding from scratch.

### STB Applications — Android TV Launcher
A specialized Android TV launcher and set-top box application for pay TV operators and telecom providers who deliver managed IPTV/hybrid services. Includes integrations for IPTV middleware, VOD platforms, electronic program guides (EPG), and operator service portals. Accedo has deep experience in the pay TV operator segment, having built STB applications for Vodafone New Zealand, Tata Play, and other operators deploying hybrid broadcast/OTT services.

### XR (Extended Reality) Applications — Xtend
Accedo's XR practice, significantly enhanced by the acquisition of EyeCandyLab. Covers augmented reality (AR) experiences overlaid on live sports video, VR immersive environments, mixed reality, and Apple Vision Pro spatial video applications. Accedo and Deutsche Telekom have showcased next-gen immersive sports streaming using XR. Collaboration with Qualcomm on XR market adoption and inclusion in the Snapdragon Spaces Pathfinder Program signals hardware-level XR integration capability.

### QoS Monitoring
An observability product built on New Relic's all-in-one observability platform, configured specifically for OTT streaming services. Monitors key components of streaming service infrastructure, end-user application operations, and video performance — providing real-time, actionable dashboards on playback quality, buffering rates, error rates by platform/device, CDN performance, and user experience metrics. Enables proactive troubleshooting before issues impact viewers. Also supports automated UX testing and experimentation at the platform level.

### End-to-End Managed Services
A comprehensive managed service offering that covers the full OTT service lifecycle — from initial strategy and launch through ongoing operations, platform management, monitoring, and continuous optimization. Accedo acts as an operational partner rather than a project vendor, with SLAs around service availability and performance. Designed for media companies that want to focus on content and business development while delegating technology operations to a specialist.

### Quality as a Managed Service
Dedicated QA/testing services tailored for OTT platforms — covering functional testing, automated regression testing, performance testing, and accessibility testing across all supported devices and platforms. Accedo employs dedicated QA engineers with device-specific expertise, reducing the need for media companies to maintain large in-house QA teams.

### FAST Channel Launch
Specialized service for designing and launching FAST (Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV) channels, including integration with FAST platform aggregators (Samsung TV Plus, LG Channels, Pluto TV, etc.), content scheduling, EPG management, and ad insertion configuration.

### Design Advisory
UX/UI strategy and design services for streaming services — including user research, information architecture, interaction design, and big-screen UI design expertise (where standard mobile/web UX patterns often fail). Accedo has designed streaming experiences for services ranging from sports-focused SVoD to multilingual broadcast services.

## Technology & Architecture
Accedo's engineering approach is deliberately technology-agnostic at the video technology layer — they integrate whatever backend systems (encoding, packaging, CDN, DRM, CMS, monetization) the client chooses or already has, rather than mandating a specific stack. This "Swiss Army knife" approach means Accedo can serve as the systems integrator across a complex, multi-vendor streaming architecture while building the application and UX layer on top.

The Elevate framework uses a modular component architecture where video playback, authentication, content browsing, search, recommendations, and payments are each independent modules — allowing clients to swap out individual components (e.g., change the recommendation engine or DRM provider) without rebuilding the entire application.

For QoS monitoring, Accedo's platform layer sits on New Relic's observability infrastructure, providing media-specific dashboards and alerting that standard New Relic configurations don't support out-of-the-box. Accedo and New Relic are formal technology partners.

For XR, the Xtend platform (from the EyeCandyLab acquisition) provides an SDK for overlaying AR content on live video streams — enabling broadcasters and rights holders to add immersive second-screen or overlay experiences without rebuilding their core streaming infrastructure.

- **Technology-agnostic integration:** Partners with competing technology vendors simultaneously — AWS and Google Cloud, Brightcove and Kaltura, multiple DRM and CDN providers — allowing Accedo to serve any client regardless of their technology stack commitments.
- **Accedo Elevate reduces launch timeline by 50%+** for new OTT services vs. building from scratch, while preserving customization flexibility that rigid white-label SaaS platforms don't allow.
- **XR capabilities through EyeCandyLab acquisition:** Most OTT services companies don't have in-house AR/VR development capability — Accedo's acquisition positions them ahead of clients' immersive experience requirements.
- **Managed services model ensures long-term relationships:** End-to-end managed services create deep client knowledge and long contract terms — many Accedo client relationships span 5+ years, building institutional knowledge that's difficult to transfer to a new vendor.
- **Sustainability practice:** Accedo actively tracks and reports on the carbon footprint of streaming service delivery, reflecting growing enterprise ESG requirements in the European media market.

- **Accedo is the specialist integrator for complex multi-platform streaming launches:** When a media company needs to ship a streaming service across 12 device platforms simultaneously, Accedo is one of the very few companies with the device-specific engineering depth to do it reliably.
- **XR is the strategic bet that differentiates from pure-play app developers:** The EyeCandyLab acquisition and Deutsche Telekom partnership position Accedo ahead of clients' immersive experience needs — few OTT services companies have this capability at all.

- **European roots mean GDPR/regulatory compliance is built-in:** For European media companies and those entering European markets, Accedo's Swedish headquarters and European client base (Channel 5, SBS, Vodafone NZ, Tata Play) reflect regulatory fluency that US-headquartered competitors sometimes lack.
