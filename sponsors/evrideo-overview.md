# Evrideo — Overview

> Broadcast Operations: Autonomized and Simplified.

## What They Do
Evrideo is a cloud-native SaaS platform that replaces traditional broadcast control rooms with a unified, cloud-hosted operating system for master control, playout, distribution, and monetization of linear, FAST, OTT, and VOD content. The company targets broadcasters, station groups, content owners, and FAST operators who are spending 80% of their budgets just keeping legacy infrastructure running and need to follow their audiences to streaming platforms — but can't afford the "Frankenstein Stack" of 15+ vendors that transition typically requires. Evrideo's answer is a single cloud platform that handles ingest, MAM, playout, graphics, scheduling, and distribution — operated as a managed service with 24/7 human support.

## Products & Services

### Broadcast Module (Core Platform)
The core operating system for broadcast operations. Handles 24/7 linear channel management, live event orchestration, content ingest, playout scheduling, and feed delivery to multiple endpoints simultaneously. Provides a single pane of glass for monitoring Linear, VOD, and FAST feeds. Guarantees 99.99%+ uptime with automated failover, multi-region cloud infrastructure, and broadcast-grade reliability.

### Cloud Master Control
The modern cloud replacement for the traditional MCR (Master Control Room). Operators monitor and manage all channels — linear, VOD, and FAST — from a unified interface. Replaces the physical control room with cloud-native equivalents of every core MCR function: signal monitoring, failover switching, QC, and distribution routing.

### Live Event Orchestration
Broadcast-grade live event management with automated failover and fault detection. Handles 24/7 live channel management for sports, news, and events. Designed for operators who cannot tolerate downtime — with a <15-minute support response time guarantee and dedicated Customer Success Managers.

### Universal Distribution Matrix
"One signal in, everywhere out." Delivers content simultaneously to satellite headends, cable head-ends, FAST platforms, CDNs, OTT apps, and social platforms from a single playout point. Supports all common broadcast/streaming outputs and distribution formats. Pre-configured templates for Roku, Samsung TV Plus, and Pluto TV eliminate manual platform-by-platform setup.

### Advanced Content Scheduling
Frame-accurate channel scheduling with automated playlist generation, dynamic content insertion, and ad placement. Supports FAST, linear, and VOD scheduling from the same scheduling interface — eliminating the need for separate scheduling tools per output type.

### Real-Time Telemetry
Built-in analytics covering viewer behavior, engagement metrics, and distribution performance. Provides operational insights alongside audience intelligence in the same platform — removing the need for a separate analytics vendor.

### Instant Channel Origination
New FAST or linear OTT channels can be launched in hours, not months. Cloud-native infrastructure with pre-built platform integrations means no hardware setup, no CDN configuration, and no lengthy ISV negotiations.

### Outpost Node
An edge computing component for hybrid deployments. Allows Evrideo to integrate with existing on-premises equipment or satellite/IP ingest points — enabling parallel deployment without ripping out current infrastructure.

### AdBoost Module
Server-side ad insertion (SSAI) capabilities for FAST and OTT channels. Enables dynamic ad insertion and monetization without requiring client-side ad SDK integration.

### SafeStream Module
Content compliance and protection module. Monitors stream health and applies compliance-related processing (e.g., loudness normalization, blackout enforcement, content substitution).

### RapidClip Module
Near-real-time clip extraction from live feeds. Allows operators to pull and publish short-form clips from live broadcasts to social and OTT platforms with minimal latency.

## Technology & Architecture
Evrideo is a true cloud-native SaaS platform — not a lift-and-shift of traditional broadcast software. The architecture supports multi-region cloud deployments and hybrid models, allowing signals to ingest from satellite, IP, and file sources and route through cloud processing before distribution to any endpoint combination.

The platform integrates all broadcast workflow stages in a single codebase: ingest → MAM → playout → graphics → scheduling → DRM → transcoding → SSAI → CDN → distribution. This monolithic-in-a-good-sense design eliminates the API handoffs, format mismatches, and timing issues that plague multi-vendor broadcast stacks. Each module (Broadcast, Outpost, AdBoost, SafeStream, RapidClip) shares a common data model and user interface.

AI autonomization is embedded across the platform rather than bolted on: automated scheduling adjusts playlists based on content availability and audience data; compliance detection identifies and handles violations without operator intervention; graphics systems auto-populate channel branding elements. Evrideo claims this AI-powered autonomization reduces manual operator workload by 60%.

The platform is designed for parallel deployment — customers can run Evrideo alongside their existing infrastructure and migrate channel by channel, rather than committing to a single big-bang replacement. This "surgical or full stack" model lowers the operational risk of cloud migration.

**Uptime:** 99.99%+ (less than 52 minutes downtime per year)
**Support:** <15-minute response time, 24/7/365, with dedicated Customer Success Managers and human broadcast engineers

## Key Differentiators
- **AI Autonomization vs. mere automation:** Evrideo explicitly distinguishes between "automated" (a human triggers a rule) and "autonomized" (the system is given an outcome goal and plans and adapts independently). This distinction positions Evrideo as AI-native, not AI-wrapped.
- **True SaaS economics:** Zero CapEx, predictable OpEx, instant scale-up or scale-down. Customers can launch a single FAST channel or migrate an entire broadcast facility — priced to match
- **42% average TCO reduction with 18-month ROI breakeven** — specific, auditable customer-reported metrics, not marketing claims
- **Platform AND a partner:** Evrideo operates as a managed broadcast service — customers get software, dedicated Customer Success Managers, and 24/7 MCR operators who function as an extension of their team. Not a self-service tool.
- **No rip-and-replace required:** Parallel deployment alongside existing infrastructure, with integration into satellite, IP, and file ingest sources already in place
