# Gracenote — Overview

> Gracenote connects people to the content they love.

## What They Do
Gracenote is the global entertainment data and metadata company, providing the comprehensive descriptive, scheduling, and identity data that powers content discovery, search, recommendations, and contextual advertising across streaming platforms, pay-TV services, consumer electronics, automotive systems, and advertising technology. They operate the world's most comprehensive entertainment metadata database — covering movies, TV shows, sports events, and music — and supply this data to Apple, Google, Samsung, Amazon, and hundreds of other platforms to make content findable and contextually useful.

## At a Glance
- **Website:** [gracenote.com](https://www.gracenote.com)
- **Industry:** Entertainment Data / Metadata / Content Intelligence
- **Stage/Positioning:** Division of Nielsen; deeply embedded in the entertainment technology ecosystem as the industry's de facto standard for content IDs and metadata

## Products & Services

### Video Data
The foundational product: comprehensive metadata for movies, TV shows, and sports programming across 80+ countries and 70+ languages. Covers titles, descriptions, cast, crew, genre taxonomy, mood and theme descriptors, ratings, and rich imagery. Includes normalized data with standardized taxonomy (unique Gracenote IDs for every content entity) that enables cross-platform interoperability. Provides listings data for 75,000+ linear TV channels and nearly 2,000 FAST channels globally.

### Content Distribution
Gracenote's metadata and ID distribution network ensures content is discoverable on leading video platforms worldwide. Content owners and distributors use Gracenote to push their metadata to major CTV platforms, streaming services, and program guides simultaneously, ensuring their titles appear with correct, rich information on every surface where viewers might find them.

### Content Tools
A portal for content owners, distributors, and studios to submit, verify, and manage their content metadata that feeds into the Gracenote system — and by extension, into the platforms that license Gracenote data. Provides the "supply side" of the metadata ecosystem.

### Advanced Discovery
AI-powered personalized content recommendation engine. Pairs Gracenote's deep descriptive metadata (mood, theme, tone, cast, narrative structure) with viewer behavior signals and contextual data to generate highly relevant, personalized recommendations. Goes beyond genre-matching to surface content based on attributes like "emotional resonance similar to what you just watched" or "same director's earlier work with similar themes."

### Sports Data
Real-time and historical sports data covering 75+ global and local sports leagues and competitions. Includes live scores, standings, schedules, team and athlete statistics, and editorial sports content. Delivered via APIs, widgets, and real-time data feeds. The On Sports product surfaces live game availability across both linear channels and streaming services simultaneously — solving the fragmented sports discovery problem where fans don't know which service is carrying a game.

**Global Sports Widgets:** Plug-and-play embeddable widgets for sports scores, standings, and schedules — deployable across web, mobile, and smart TV without custom development.

**On Sports:** Links to live games across linear and streaming, with statistics and related content in a unified sports experience, enabling platforms to show viewers exactly which service is carrying tonight's game.

### Streaming and Linear Availability
Shows viewers where to find specific content across all providers, platforms, and services — both SVOD and linear — in a unified availability layer. Addresses the frustration of fragmented discovery where a viewer knows what they want to watch but doesn't know which of their subscriptions carries it.

### Content Analytics
Data and insights products for content creators and distributors to make informed business decisions:
- **Studio System:** Tracks entertainment industry projects and people across film and TV to identify talent trends and inform content acquisition
- **Inclusion Analytics:** Diversity and representation data across programming to support DE&I-informed content investment decisions
- **Contextual Video Data:** Deep content attribute data for advertising use cases — enabling contextual targeting based on what's happening in a scene, not just who the viewer is

### Audio Data
Music metadata for over 100 million tracks, albums, and artists. Powers music discovery, auto-identification (Gracenote MusicID® — the industry standard for music recognition), and playlist personalization. Covers podcast metadata as well.

### Nexus Auto
Combines audio, sports, and video metadata to deliver in-car entertainment experiences. Powers infotainment systems for automotive manufacturers, providing the metadata backbone for connected car entertainment, visual radio, and in-car content discovery.

### Gracenote Video MCP Server
A new product enabling AI systems (LLMs and AI agents) to query Gracenote's video data in real time via the Model Context Protocol — the emerging standard for grounding AI responses in live, accurate data. Allows AI-powered CTV search and recommendation systems to access current Gracenote metadata without pre-downloading static data lakes.

- **Automotive Entertainment:** Powering in-car infotainment metadata for visual radio, connected car content discovery, and driver personalization
- **Content Analytics:** Helping studios track programming trends, distribution coverage, and diversity representation to inform investment decisions

## Technology & Architecture
Gracenote operates a massive editorial + AI hybrid metadata production system. Human editors maintain the quality and completeness of core entertainment data across 80+ countries (particularly for local and regional programming), while AI/ML systems handle volume tasks: auto-classifying content attributes, detecting duplicates, and propagating metadata enrichment at scale.

The Gracenote ID system is the core technical infrastructure: unique, stable identifiers for every content entity (title, episode, character, person, sports team, athlete, league) that remain consistent across markets and platforms. These IDs are the "common key" that enables content metadata to be matched across different platforms, advertising systems, and data providers — solving the interoperability problem that makes cross-platform measurement and targeting possible in programmatic CTV.

For contextual advertising, Gracenote's content descriptors go significantly deeper than primary genre: they include mood (tense, lighthearted, emotional), theme (family, redemption, competition), scenario descriptors, and character attributes — enabling advertisers to target based on what's happening in a scene, not just the show category.

The Video MCP Server product represents Gracenote's bet on AI-native architecture: rather than requiring platforms to periodically sync large metadata datasets, the MCP server enables LLMs and AI agents to query live Gracenote data at inference time — grounding AI recommendations with current, accurate information about what's available, when, and where.

Gracenote's 2025 State of Play report (based on proprietary research) found that nearly 50% of U.S. TV viewers would consider canceling a streaming service because they can't find something to watch — validating metadata's business-critical role in retention.

## Key Differentiators
- **Industry-standard content IDs:** Gracenote IDs are the de facto identifier standard across the entertainment ecosystem — enabling cross-platform advertising measurement, content matching, and attribution in ways that require no other vendor to also be in the room
- **Global scale with local depth:** Coverage across 80+ countries and 70+ languages, including local and regional content that global aggregators frequently miss — critical for international streaming services
- **FAST channel metadata leadership:** Gracenote tracks nearly 2,000 FAST channels globally and is actively working to solve the FAST metadata gap that blocks programmatic ad revenue (as documented in their research showing only 43% of sports FAST programming includes original air date in submission)
- **Sports discovery at the moment of need:** The On Sports product bridges the linear/streaming divide for live sports availability — at the moment 65% of TV viewers say live sports is a reason to pay for a service, helping users find those games is a direct revenue enabler
- **LLM grounding via MCP:** The Video MCP Server positions Gracenote's data as the grounding layer for AI-powered entertainment experiences — a forward-looking architectural bet as CTV platforms explore LLM-powered search and recommendations

- **LLM grounding is the next frontier:** The Video MCP Server product positions Gracenote as the data layer for the AI era — not just a traditional data licensing business but the metadata infrastructure that makes AI-powered entertainment experiences accurate and trustworthy
