# Persistent Systems — Overview

> Re(AI)magining the World — A Trusted Digital Engineering and Enterprise Modernization Partner

## What They Do
Persistent Systems is a global digital engineering and IT services company — publicly traded in India — that builds software products, modernizes enterprise platforms, and delivers AI-native solutions for industries ranging from banking to healthcare to media. In the streaming and media context, Persistent's Media, Entertainment & Gaming practice builds and modernizes OTT platforms, broadcast infrastructure, digital advertising technology, and media supply chains for studios, streamers, broadcasters, and gaming organizations. Their positioning at the NAB Streaming Summit is as a technology engineering partner for media companies navigating the transition from legacy broadcast infrastructure to cloud-native, AI-driven streaming platforms.

## Products & Services

### Persistent.AI
Persistent's flagship AI practice and delivery framework — branded as "Re(AI)magining" — covering generative AI, agentic AI, large language model integration, and AI-powered automation across all service lines. For media companies, Persistent.AI applications include AI newsroom assistants with editorial guardrails, GenAI-powered content clipping/captioning/localization tools for creator platforms, predictive analytics for audience churn and subscription propensity, and agentic AI for ad-ops automation (planning, pacing, and yield optimization). The practice also covers AI infrastructure on NVIDIA-powered GPU systems and integrations with Databricks, Snowflake, and hyperscaler AI services.

### OTT & Streaming Solutions
End-to-end engineering for OTT platform launches and modernizations — covering SVoD, AVoD, and FAST channel factories. Services include: low-latency live streaming infrastructure design and implementation, quality-of-experience (QoE) optimization, FinOps for cloud encoding and delivery cost reduction (targeting 20–30% spend reduction), vector-search-driven personalization and recommendation engines, privacy-safe audience data activation, converged ad-tech integration, and ABR bitrate tuning. Persistent claims typical timelines of 8–12 weeks to launch new features or products vs. conventional quarters-long timelines.

### Broadcast & Live Production Engineering
Migrating broadcast playout and live production workflows to cloud: channel origination in the cloud, remote/virtual production infrastructure, automated compliance workflows, and converged linear-to-digital ad operations. This includes disaster recovery planning for live events and cross-screen monetization strategies that bridge linear broadcast and digital streaming delivery.

### Digital Advertising & AdTech
Building outcome-based, privacy-first advertising products: universal selling platforms, clean-room audience graphs, fraud and transparency controls (IVT/supply path optimization), shoppable video integration, and agentic ad-ops automation. Claimed outcome: 8–15% ARPU lift via privacy-safe targeting and outcome-based CTV packages.

### Publishing, News Media & Creator Economy
Subscription propensity modeling and churn-save systems, headless CMS modernization for edge delivery, AI newsroom assistants with editorial guardrails, trust and safety systems for UGC platforms, creator onboarding and payout compliance, and cross-title data monetization frameworks for creator marketplaces.

### Gaming & Interactive Entertainment
Backend infrastructure for multiplayer games (authentication, matchmaking, telemetry, anti-cheat), LiveOps personalization for game-as-a-service titles, cloud-native GaaS with observability and FinOps, and governed generative AI tools for in-game content creation.

### Software Product Engineering
Custom software development and platform engineering as an outsourced/extended engineering team for product companies. This is historically Persistent's core offering — building software on behalf of ISVs and platform companies. The Empirix case study shows 50% reduction in time-to-market; Fusion Connect case study shows accelerated development of hybrid workforce solutions.

### Cloud & Infrastructure Services
Cloud migration, infrastructure modernization, and managed cloud services across AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, and private cloud environments. Persistent is a partner of all three hyperscalers.

### Enterprise Integration & Application Development
API integration, enterprise application modernization (legacy-to-cloud lift/shift and re-platform), and middleware engineering for connecting disparate enterprise systems.

## Technology & Architecture
Persistent delivers services using three architectural pillars. First, **Hyperscaler-native delivery** — pre-built accelerators, reference architectures, and integration blueprints co-developed with AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft that allow media companies to use proven cloud-native patterns rather than starting from scratch. Second, **AI-native engineering** — Persistent.AI is not a layer on top of existing services but integrated into every engagement; they use agentic workflows, GenAI code generation, and AI-assisted testing to accelerate development velocity. Third, **Open Source Hub** — Persistent maintains an open source practice that contributes to and builds on the open source ecosystem, relevant for media companies using open-source streaming tooling like FFmpeg, GStreamer, or OpenCDN specifications.

Persistent has built Private 5G Lab infrastructure for testing telecom edge scenarios, and has deep experience with OSS/BSS modernization for telco customers — providing relevant overlap for streaming companies working with telecom distribution partners.
